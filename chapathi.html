Chapathi Recipe

Estimated Calorie Intake: 120 Calories

Health Benefits:

  • Weight management
  • Fiber boost
  • Aids digestion

Preparation Steps:

  1. Roll out balls from the prepared dough.
  2. Flatten them with a rolling pin while using flour to prevent sticking.
  3. Place a pan on medium heat and cook the chapathi on both sides.
  4. Use tongs to flip and ensure even cooking.
  5. Once brown spots appear, remove and keep warm.
