Chicken Salad Recipe

Estimated Calorie Intake: 500 Calories

Health Benefits:

  • High in protein
  • Nutrient-dense and healthy

Preparation Steps:

  1. Prepare ingredients: shred pre-cooked chicken, chop grapes, lemon juice, onion, celery, and dill.
  2. Mix all ingredients with mayo, Dijon, and celery seeds.
  3. Ensure even coating and mix well.
  4. Chill in the fridge before serving.
