Dosa Recipe

Name of Recipe: Dosa

Estimated Calorie Intake: 100 Kilocalories

Health Benefits of Recipe:

  • Aids in promoting gut health
  • Rich in vitamins A and C
  • Rich in iron and calcium

Preparation:

  1. Add enough water to the fermented batter until you have a smooth, pouring consistency.
  2. Make sure the batter is not too watery; it should be thick but fall off your spoon easily.
  3. Heat a tawa or iron griddle and lightly grease with oil. Pour about 1/2 cup batter in the center.
  4. Using the back of your ladle, gently spread the batter in circular motions from the center outward.
  5. Don't spread it too thin; the dosa should be evenly thick.
  6. Drizzle about 1 tsp of oil around the edges and add a few sprinkles on the top.
  7. When the edges start to lift, use a spatula to flip the dosa.
  8. Cook for another 40 seconds and remove from the pan.
  9. Serve with dosa podi, chutney, sambar, or anything of your choice!
