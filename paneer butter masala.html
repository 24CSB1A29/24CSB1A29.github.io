Paneer Butter Masala Recipe

1. Ripe, red & juicy tomatoes form the base of the makhani sauce. Use good, ripe tomatoes or canned whole tomatoes for the best flavor.

2. Raw cashews add creaminess and balance the tanginess of the tomatoes.

3. Cream enhances the richness of the dish. Coconut cream can be used as a substitute for a vegan version.

4. Butter provides a luxurious, buttery texture to the curry.

5. Paneer should be fresh and soft to absorb the flavors of the dish well.

6. Spices like Kashmiri red chili powder, garam masala, and dried fenugreek leaves (kasuri methi) add depth to the flavor.

7. Garnish with fresh cilantro for a refreshing touch.
