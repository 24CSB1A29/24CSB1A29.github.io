Idly Recipe

Estimated Calorie Intake: 61 Kilocalories

Health Benefits:

  • Low in calories
  • Helps control sugar levels
  • Provides healthy carbohydrates
  • Easily digestible

Preparation Steps:

  1. Soak rice, poha, and urad dal separately for 4-5 hours.
  2. Grind urad dal into a smooth batter.
  3. Grind rice and poha together and mix with urad dal batter.
  4. Let the batter ferment overnight.
  5. Steam in greased idly molds for 12-15 minutes.
  6. Serve hot with sambar and coconut chutney.
